Who Am I?

I work to understand the needs and perspectives of users and evaluate appropriate solutions for them. Over the years, I have encountered interfaces that complicate work for my users. I can provide them with all of the workarounds they want, but it frustrates me when the software limits them. My goal is to transform user interfaces to make users more efficient and excited about the software they use!

Experiences

As a student, I took INLS 718: User Interface Design and learned the principles for creating the interface designs of information systems. I also learned how to develop user personas and stories, use cases, descriptions of design issues, and evaluate usability case studies. During class projects, I hosted and participated in a variety of usability testing sessions which included A/B testing and hallway testing methods.

Why Should You Hire Me?

My co-workers would say that I am quick to pick up on new concepts and tools and immediately ready to put them into practical use. I am a user-centric professional-- people often come to me when they want someone to explain technical concepts in a digestible way.
