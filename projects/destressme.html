De-StressMe : a mobile app to manage stress

Download the app

Project Roles: UX/UI Designer

System Scope

De-StressMe is a Android app that is intended to make mental health treatment more accessible to people who may not have the ability to receive mental health treatment. It is built upon the principles of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy.

I developed this app in partnership with a Psychiatry Resident who wanted a simple design that users could easily understand and feel at peace while using.

I proposed a grid layout, which clearly defined each action that the user could take on the app. I also chose a calming color palette in order to ease the user's mind. Users would be using this app to calm themselves down after an anxiety-inducing or depressive episode. Therefore, the UI was created to be a worry-free experience.

Design Decisions

White space was use liberally in the app in order to give the user chance to process their feelings. I purposely avoided putting more text on the screen than was necessary in order to allow users to free themselves from life's constant bombardment of information.
